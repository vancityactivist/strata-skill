# CHOA Guidance Index

The Condominium Home Owners Association of BC (CHOA, choa.bc.ca) has published practical strata guidance since 1976 — most of it written or curated by Tony Gioventu. Where the SPA says *what the law is*, CHOA bulletins say *how it plays out in practice*: real scenarios, checklists, and the failure modes agents actually encounter.

## How to use this index

**Do not reproduce CHOA content.** Every bulletin states "No part of this publication may be reproduced without the prior written permission of CHOA." Instead:

1. When a question lands in one of the topic areas below, fetch the relevant bulletin live from choa.bc.ca (search the bulletins list at `choa.bc.ca/resources/choa-information-bulletins-list/` by topic keyword or bulletin number).
2. Use it to inform your answer, and cite it ("see CHOA bulletin 400-016") so the agent can pull the original — CHOA membership gives full access.
3. Treat bulletins as practice guidance, not law. Older bulletins (pre-2023 especially) may predate the Bill 44 rental changes, the CRF 10% rule, and the depreciation-report reforms — always reconcile against `strata-property-act.md` and the current consolidated act.

CHOA's own legislative-updates page (`choa.bc.ca/resources/strata-property-act/legislative-updates/`) lags the current year; use it for history, not currency.

## Bulletin numbering

- **200-xxx** — case studies, research, policy analysis
- **300-xxx** — Condo Smarts Q&A columns and topical deep-dives
- **400-xxx** — checklists, templates, procedural guides (the most directly actionable series)
- **600-xxx** — comprehensive guides
- **700-xxx** — legislative bulletins
- **800-xxx** — Q&A on specific owner/council concerns
