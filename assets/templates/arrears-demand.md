# Template: Arrears Demand Letter

## Usage notes (not part of the letter)

- Position in the escalation ladder: friendly reminder → **this letter** → Form G Certificate of Lien (s. 116) → CRT/court.
- Only include the lien warning if the arrears are actually lienable: strata fees, special levies, s. 85 reimbursements, or a share of a judgment. **Fines and remediation costs are not lienable (s. 116(3))** — for those, the escalation is a CRT claim, and the letter must say so instead. Do not threaten a lien you cannot register; that itself creates CRT exposure.
- s. 112 requires 2 weeks' written demand before registering a lien — this letter satisfies that if it gives at least 14 days.
- Itemize the arrears by type and date so the owner (and later a tribunal) can see exactly what is owed.
- Interest is only chargeable if a bylaw or the special levy resolution provides for it, within the Regulation's cap.
