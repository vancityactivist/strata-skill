# Template: Notice of Annual General Meeting

## Usage notes (not part of the letter)

- Timing: the AGM must be held within 2 months of fiscal year end (s. 40). Notice must be given at least 14 days before the meeting (s. 45), **plus 4 days deemed-delivery time if sent by mail, fax, or email (s. 61)** — so mail at least 18 days out, and build in a buffer beyond that.
- The notice must include: the agenda, the proposed budget, the financial statement, and the **full proposed text** of any resolution requiring a 3/4 or unanimous vote (s. 45(3)-(4)). A 3/4-vote resolution merely summarized in the notice is challengeable.
- Send to all owners, and to tenants/mortgagees who have given notice of entitlement (s. 45(1)).
- Quorum is eligible voters holding 1/3 of votes, in person or by proxy (s. 48) — remind owners to submit proxies if they can't attend.
- Electronic attendance is permitted without a special bylaw since the November 2022 (Bill 44) amendments, unless the bylaws restrict it; include connection details if offered.
